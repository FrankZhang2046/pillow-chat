## 1. Strategic Premise

The NSFW AI companion market has commoditized models — everyone rents the same LLMs. Durable advantage comes from **distribution + retention mechanics**, and this plan is built around both:

- **Positioning wedge:** *"The companion that actually remembers you."* Memory/relationship-continuity is the product's validated differentiator, it's demo-able in a screenshot, and it targets the #1 complaint users have about churn-and-burn competitors.
- **Channel thesis:** buyers in this niche search and lurk; they respond to being findable, not being pitched. High-intent search volume is large ("AI girlfriend," "[competitor] alternative," long-tail fantasy/persona queries). An inbound machine matches both the market and the operator's demonstrated strengths (content cadence, SEO-shaped publishing, YouTube-honed hooks).
- **Time model:** near-silent for 2–3 months, then compounding — the 结硬寨打呆仗 wager. Accepted explicitly; runway covers it. The weekly publishing quota is therefore the single load-bearing commitment: the system has no other input.

## 2. Channel Architecture

### Primary — SEO (~70% of effort)
The engine and eventual moat. Three content tiers, all on a marketing site + blog owned outright:

| Tier | Content | Intent | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Comparison | "[Competitor] alternatives," "apps like X," head-to-heads | Highest | Intercept existing demand at decision moment |
| Long-tail | Persona/use-case/fantasy pages, programmatic where sensible | High | Low-competition volume aggregation |
| Educational | How AI memory works, model comparisons, build write-ups | Medium | Domain authority; earns organic links from the RP community without outreach |

Notes:
- Educational tier double-dips: the AI RP community shares genuinely good technical content on its own — inbound link-building.
- Stand up the site **before** product launch; domain age and indexing lead time make every early week a head start.

### Secondary — X/Twitter (~25% of effort)
The one mainstream platform permitting adult content (with labels). Pure broadcast mode:
- Build-in-public threads (context pipeline, memory system), memory-demo clips, persona showcases
- Batched weekly, scheduled via Buffer/Typefully; no engagement farming, no reply labor
- Functions as the discovery surface for the AI-companion scene and the hub linking to site + affiliate page

### Tertiary — Reddit (~5% of effort)
Presence, not a worked channel:
- Launch-day posts in AI RP / NSFW chatbot subs with self-promo threads
- Answer inbound questions; nothing proactive
- Primary value: SEO/educational content gets shared into these communities by others

## 3. Passive Fixtures (build once, run forever)

1. **Self-serve affiliate program** — public signup page, 25% recurring rev-share, footer link + pinned X post. Converts the entire "best NSFW AI" listicle/directory ecosystem into commissioned distribution without a single pitch email. Cost only exists when revenue does. Ship as soon as billing exists.
2. **Directory submissions** — one afternoon submitting to NSFW AI directories/aggregators via their forms. One-time flag-planting where traffic already flows.
3. **In-product referral loop** — invite → both parties receive bonus messages. In a habit-forming product, users become the acquisition channel structurally. Zero marginal cost.
4. **Freemium message cap** — the pricing model is itself the top-of-funnel: free tier acquires, memory continuity converts.

## 4. Weekly Operating Cadence

| Output | Qty | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| SEO pages | 2 | Rotate tiers; comparison pages prioritized first 8 weeks |
| X posts | 3 | Scheduled; batch-produced |
| Demo clip | 1 | Memory feature front and center; doubles as X + site asset |

Batch production day recommended (mirrors Vim Sensei operation). This quota sits on the same calendar as existing content commitments and is treated as non-negotiable — consistency is the entire mechanism.

## 5. Sequencing

**Phase 0 — Pre-launch (now):** register domain, stand up marketing site + blog, begin SEO quota immediately, open X account, start build-in-public queue.
**Phase 1 — Launch:** Reddit launch posts, directory submissions, referral loop live.
**Phase 2 — Billing live:** affiliate page up + pinned; affiliates and listicles onboard themselves.
**Phase 3 — Months 3–6:** SEO compounding begins; double down on whichever tier shows traction; consider expanding programmatic long-tail.
**Deferred indefinitely:** paid adult ad networks (TrafficJunky/ExoClick) — revisit only with proven LTV and appetite for paid; community hustle/outreach — permanently out of scope by design.

## 6. Hard Constraints & Hygiene

- **Identity firewall:** separate legal entity, domains, emails, accounts, analytics. No cross-links, no shared imagery, no voice overlap with Vim Sensei or personal brand. Treat as unbreakable.
- **Closed channels (do not attempt):** Google/Meta/TikTok ads, app stores, Patreon/Gumroad/Ko-fi, mainstream sponsorships. Workarounds risk frozen funds and platform bans.
- **X labeling compliance:** mark account/media as adult per platform policy; violation risks the 25% channel.
- **Claims hygiene:** demo clips use fictional personas only; no real-person likenesses anywhere in marketing.

## 7. Metrics That Matter

| Phase | North star | Supporting |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-launch | Pages indexed | Impressions (Search Console) |
| Launch–M3 | Signups/week | X profile visits → site CTR; referral participation rate |
| M3+ | Organic signups/week from SEO | Free→paid conversion; affiliate-driven signups; D30 retention (validates the positioning promise) |

Retention is listed as a *marketing* metric deliberately: "she remembers you" is a claim the product must keep for word-of-mouth and referral mechanics to function.

## 8. Risks & Honest Trade-offs

- **Slow ramp is structural.** Inbound-only forfeits the launch spike. Mitigation: none needed — accepted trade against runway; the quota is the hedge.
- **Single-channel concentration (SEO).** Google volatility is real in adult-adjacent niches. Mitigation: X as independent discovery surface; affiliate/referral loops as non-Google traffic; email capture on the free tier from day one (owned audience).
- **Quota decay.** Historical weakness is execution stamina. Mitigation: same countermeasure as Vim Sensei — fixed calendar, batch days, publicly scheduled queue; the plan intentionally has one commitment, not five.
- **Positioning replication.** Memory claims are copyable in marketing copy; defensible only via actual product superiority — which loops back to the memory system remaining the engineering priority.
